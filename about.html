Flight Log: Our History

2018

Initial Takeoff

Started with a single Cessna 172 in a shared hangar in New York, focusing on curated local sunrise tours.

2021

Alpine Expansion

Established our European hub in the Swiss Alps with mountain-certified crew and specialized high-altitude aircraft.

2026

Global Network

Now operating across 12 countries with a fleet of 25 premium aircraft, serving over 50,000 yearly explorers.
