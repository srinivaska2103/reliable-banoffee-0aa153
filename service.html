Common Questions

Everything you need to know before you take to the skies.

Are scenic flights safe?
Safety is our DNA. We follow strict international aviation standards. Every aircraft undergoes a multi-point inspection daily, and our pilots are mountain-flight certified with thousands of hours of experience.
What happens in bad weather?
We monitor real-time telemetry. If conditions are unsafe or visibility is poor, we will notify you at least 2 hours before takeoff to reschedule or provide a full refund. Your safety and the quality of the view are our priorities.
Can I bring my professional camera?
Absolutely! We encourage it. Our aircraft windows are specifically cleaned and designed for anti-glare photography. We also offer "Doors-Off" flights for professional photographers upon request.
What is the cancellation policy?
You can cancel or reschedule free of charge up to 48 hours before your flight. Cancellations within 24-48 hours incur a 50% fee, and "no-shows" are non-refundable.

Still have questions? Our ground crew is ready to help.
